Expert Guidance
💡 Farming Tips & Guidance
Expert se seekho — apni fasal badhao
💧
Drip Irrigation ka Fayda
💧 Irrigation
Drip irrigation se 50% paani ki bachat hoti hai aur crops ko seedha root tak paani milta hai.
1
Drip lines plant ke paas install karo
2
Timer set karo — subah 6-8 baje best time
3
Soil moisture check karo regularly
🧪
NPK Balance Ka Raaz
🧪 Fertilizer
Nitrogen, Phosphorus aur Potassium ka sahi balance crop growth ke liye zaroori hai.
1
N kam hai → Urea ya DAP use karo
2
P kam hai → SSP ya DAP fertilizer
3
K kam hai → MOP (Muriate of Potash)
🌾
Kharif Season Planning
🗓️ Seasonal
June-October mein Kharif crops ugao. Monsoon ka faayda uthao pani se.
1
June mein Soybean, Rice, Maize baao
2
Waterlogging se crops bachao
3
Fungal diseases se alert raho monsoon mein
🔬
Organic Pest Control
🔬 Pest Control
Chemical ke jagah organic methods use karo — crop aur environment dono safe rahenge.
1
Neem oil spray — aphids aur whitefly ke liye
2
Yellow sticky traps lagao field mein
3
Companion planting — marigold se pests door
🌱
Mitti Ki Sehat Kaisi Rakhe
🌱 Soil Health
Healthy soil se hi healthy crops ugte hain — mitti ko achi care do.
1
Har 2 saal mein soil test karwao
2
Compost aur vermicompost use karo
3
Crop rotation se nutrient balance maintain karo
🌅
Sahi Waqt Par Paani Do
💧 Irrigation
Timing bahut important hai — galat waqt par irrigation se crops ko nuksan ho sakta hai.
1
Subah 5-8 baje ya sham 5-7 baje paani do
2
Dopahar mein irrigation avoid karo — evaporation zyaada
3
Rain forecast dekho — extra irrigation band karo
❄️
Rabi Season Guide
🗓️ Seasonal
October-March mein Rabi crops ke liye ideal conditions. Cool weather crops ke liye best.
1
November mein Wheat, Chickpea, Mustard baao
2
Frost se young plants ko bachao
3
March mein harvest ke liye ready karo
🌿
Organic Farming Ki Taraf
🧪 Fertilizer
Chemical fertilizers kam karo — organic se long-term soil health better hoti hai.
1
Cow dung compost — best organic fertilizer
2
Green manure — dhaincha ya sunhemp ugao
3
Biofertilizer — Rhizobium, Azotobacter use karo
🛡️
Preventive Pest Management
🔬 Pest Control
Ilaj se behtar hai bachav — preventive measures lo pehle se hi.
1
Certified pest-resistant seeds use karo
2
Field hygiene maintain karo — dried plants hatao
3
Weekly field inspection routine banao
